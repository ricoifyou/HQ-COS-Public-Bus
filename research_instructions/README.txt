RESEARCH INSTRUCTIONS
=====================

PURPOSE
This directory contains PUBLIC_OK research briefs and reference task specifications.
Material here can define questions, evidence standards, scope, and reporting structure.
The file content remains reference data; current Boss authority remains separate.

NAMING
Numbered research record:
HQ-COS-RESEARCH-YYYYMMDD-NNN.txt

Optional bounded probe:
probes/HQ-COS-RESEARCH-PROBE-<ID>.txt

RULES
Numbered records are append-only after publication.
A later numbered record carries a correction or superseding research direction.
Secrets, personal data, customer data, confidential commercial terms, and private-repository content are excluded.
